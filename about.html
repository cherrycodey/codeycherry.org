About

Hi!
My name is Codey Cherry. I am a resident of Montgomery, Alabama. My family once owned and operated many busineses in the greater Montgomery area. Two that are probably well known are Cherry's and Cherry's Recycling. My mother (Eloise) and father (Al) started the market and everything contiued to grow until the death of my brother (Jeff).


I currently work for the state of Alabama, Monday - Friday, 7:00 AM - 4:00 PM. My job title is IT Systems Specialist. I am also an on call carpet technician. On top of that I maintain all of our family properties and rental locations. Very busy man!


I am blessed to be a father to Faith. Her, my mother, and my father have molded me into the man I am today. Which if you ask people that know me well, they will say that I'm a great guy. However if you ask people I immediatley work with, such as my supervisor, you may hear something else, which is 100% not true.


I was raised in the old school manner. I'm well versed in respect, manners, and trust. I have a very strong work ethic, thanks to my family. I work hard and I give my all in everything that I do. I am a professional,who is polite, and working toward a better tomorrow. I am constantly working on being the best version of me. I think I've blown my own horn enough now.
